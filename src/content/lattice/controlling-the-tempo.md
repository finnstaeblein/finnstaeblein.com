Buyers optimize for optionality; without pressure, decisions slip indefinitely. Controlling the tempo means manufacturing legitimate scarcity and deadlines — a limited cohort, a real date — so the path of least resistance stops being "wait until next quarter."

**Why it matters:** you rarely lose to a competitor; you lose to "no decision." Remove the option to stall for free.
