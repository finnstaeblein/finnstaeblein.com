As agents produce more consequential work, someone neutral has to certify it — and the value comes precisely from having no stake in the outcome. There are precedents for a trusted, agenda-free third party becoming essential infrastructure: identity (Okta), credit ratings (Moody's), even talent appeal scores (the Q Score) in Hollywood.

**Why it matters:** neutrality is the product. Whoever becomes the trusted, disinterested certifier of agentic output sits in a structurally strong position.
