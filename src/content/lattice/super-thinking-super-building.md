Two founder qualities worth weighting heavily when you back someone:

- **Super thinking** — reasoning many steps ahead; seeing where a decision leads ten moves out, not just the next one.
- **Super building** — the willingness and speed to throw an idea away and rebuild it from scratch when the evidence says so, instead of defending sunk work.

**Why it matters:** the first tells you they can read the board; the second tells you they'll adapt when the board changes. A founder with both compounds — and compounding is the whole game.
