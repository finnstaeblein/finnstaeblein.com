Every platform wave makes something abundant and pushes value onto the newly-scarce complement. Cheap computers made hardware abundant and software the scarce, valuable layer. Cheap distribution over the internet made content abundant and attention scarce. The pattern repeats: find what the wave makes abundant, then look for the complement it makes rare.

**Why it matters:** to locate the next monopoly, don't chase the abundant thing everyone can now do — build the scarce complement it creates. When inference becomes abundant, bet the scarcity is in trust and proof-of-work.
